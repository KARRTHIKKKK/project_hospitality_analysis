### 📖 Project Overview
AtliQ Grands, a luxury hotel chain in India, is facing a decline in market share and revenue due to strategic moves by competitors and ineffective decision-making. This project involves developing a data-driven solution to provide the Revenue Management team with actionable insights. 

By leveraging **Python**, this analysis identifies trends in booking patterns, room utilization, and pricing integrity to help the chain regain its competitive edge.

### 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Language:** Python
* **Libraries:** Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn
* **Tools:** Jupyter Notebook / VS Code / Git

### 📈 Key Hospitality KPIs
The analysis focuses on five essential metrics to evaluate property performance:
* **RevPAR (Revenue Per Available Room):** Measures the ability to generate revenue from total available inventory.
* **ADR (Average Daily Rate):** Evaluates the average price paid for occupied rooms.
* **Occupancy %:** Tracks the percentage of successful room stays over a specific period.
* **Realization %:** Measures the ratio of "Checked Out" bookings vs. total bookings to identify cancellation trends.
* **DSRN (Daily Sellable Room Nights):** Analyzes the capacity available for sale each day.

### 💡 Key Insights Generated
* **Revenue by Category:** Identified which room types (Luxury vs. Business) drive the highest margins.
* **City Performance:** Analyzed performance across Mumbai, Delhi, Hyderabad, and Bangalore.
* **Platform Efficiency:** Tracked booking success rates across various OTAs (Online Travel Agencies) and direct bookings.
* **Weekend vs. Weekday Trends:** Highlighted significant spikes in occupancy during weekends, suggesting dynamic pricing opportunities.
